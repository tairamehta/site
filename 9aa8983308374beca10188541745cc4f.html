The Gratitude Project is centred around raising awareness + taking action against the rising mental health epidemic around high schoolers during quarantined periods. We use our online platform @TheGratitudeProject101 as an outlet to raise awareness for methods of gratitude that can help defeat the root cause of low mental health amongst high schoolers during the pandemic. As a 100% online initiative, we are focused on raising awareness, taking action in the form of online Movie nights, Trivia nights, games nights etc. as a means to connect the community and reinforce the idea that "no one is alone".
